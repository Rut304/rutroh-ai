RutRoh Inc. ("Company," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our websites and services, including rutroh.org, the Matchups platform, and associated pages (the "Services").

  • Communications you send us via email.

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device type, browser type, operating system, and IP address.
  • Pages visited, time spent, referring URLs, and interaction patterns.
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies for analytics and functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our Services.

We use third-party services that may collect information:

5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Authenticate your session for premium features.
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns.
  • Support affiliate tracking for product recommendations.

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function properly without them.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Secure credential storage (API keys in environment variables, not in source code).
  • Regular security audits and monitoring.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at rutrohd@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. International Users

Our Services are operated from the United States. If you access our Services from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from your jurisdiction.
