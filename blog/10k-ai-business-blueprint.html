AI AGENTS March 1, 2026 · 6 min read

Introduction

From $0 to $10K/month, our journey with AI-driven businesses has been transformative. Here’s how we did it.

Phase 1: Content Creation

We started by automating blog posts and social media. AI agents handled research, writing, and scheduling. Affiliate links like The Lean Startup (affiliate link) provided early revenue.

Phase 2: Trading Bots

Next, we built AI trading bots for Kalshi. With a 65% win rate, this became a steady income stream.

Phase 3: Scaling

We diversified into niche websites, AI tutoring, and freelance services. Automation was key.

Phase 4: SaaS Products

With steady revenue, we developed SaaS tools for others to replicate our success.

Conclusion

The path to $10K/month is paved with automation and scalability. Start small, automate, and grow.
